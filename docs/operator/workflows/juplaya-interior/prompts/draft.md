# Draft — Interior / layout decisions

Use case: convertible bikes-out -> sleeping deck, hauling a WR250R + CRF450RL (~590 lb). Interior 81" W x 157" L x 6'6" tall. ~(6) 10 ft black powder-coated E-track sections believed on hand. 2"x4" steel main rails; 4x factory 5,000 lb floor D-rings; 3/4" PlexCore floor.

Resolve each open question:

1. **E-track layout** — floor-row orientation (side-by-side vs single-file for the two bikes), row spacing (tie to front-tire centerlines), and mounting method (flush-recessed vs surface; bolt through to the steel crossmembers vs plywood only).
2. **Horizontal track heights** — set the two horizontal-track heights for a bed + a shelf in the 6'6" interior; survey comparable builds and reason from sit-up clearance over the bed (bikes-out, so nothing must clear under it) and a usable shelf above a seated head.
3. **Bed shoring beams** — aluminum shoring beams vs E-track inserts that accept 2x4 lumber. Recommend.
4. **Wall finish + build-up** — leaning FRP (Home Depot textured panel); could be talked into luan; NO paint; maybe a 1/4" XPS sheet as a thermal break. Recommend the wall build-up spec + trim options.
5. **Personnel-door stairs** — recommend stairs for the 32"x72" side door.

**Sources:** read `docs/juplaya-trailer-context.md` (context + open questions) and the confirmed specs in `docs/fastrac-specs.md` + `docs/wells-cargo-ft712s2-d-work-order.md`.
