# Trailer — Mission

## North star

> **A reconfigurable mobile platform that hauls and supports whatever the mission is, and can live off-grid for days. The track grid is the operating system; the interior is software.**

Same shell, swap the mission by reconfiguring the interior. Nothing permanently bolted. Floor L-track and wall E-track form the universal mounting grid — every fit-out repositions on it.

The shell is fixed: insulated, ventilated (HRV), heated (diesel), CO-monitored, powered (48V LiTime stack), wired through ENT with pull strings for whatever comes next. That base makes it livable and safe across every profile below.

**Decided constraints:** generator handles metal-fab power demand (the battery alone won't sustain welding). Weight is accepted as a managed tradeoff, not a hard limiter.

## Mission profiles

### 1. Moto basecamp
Haul two bikes (WR250R + CRF450RL) to a remote ride, unload, convert interior to sleeping deck, live out of it.
- Track: lengthwise floor L-track rows + chocks for the bikes; wall E-track rows for the bed/shelf deck.
- Power: sips — lights, fans, charging. Battery alone is plenty.
- First proof: **Juplaya** (~July 4 2026).

### 2. Mobile metal fab
Carry fab kit to a worksite; weld/fabricate on location.
- Track grid: secures welder cart, gas bottle(s) vertically, tool chest, steel stock.
- Power: **generator** for sustained TIG/MIG; battery covers light/field tacking off-grid.
- Safety: HRV + CO detector already cover shielding-gas / engine-adjacent environment.

### 3. Kayaking expedition basecamp
Stage a paddling trip — gear haul + shelter at put-in/camp.
- Track grid: boat racks, paddle/PFD storage, wet/dry gear separation.
- Power: charging, lights, drying/ventilation.

### 4. Offroad / Moab basecamp
Tow behind the F-150 to remote trailheads; basecamp for multi-day wheeling.
- Track grid: recovery gear, spares, tools, fuel/water securement.
- Power + livability: full off-grid stay between runs.

## What stays constant across all profiles
- **Track grid** — floor L-track for low-profile tie-down/chock duty; wall E-track for bed/shelf/modules.
- **Off-grid livability stack** — LiFePO4 power, HRV, diesel heat, insulation, CO detector.
- **F-150 tow**, single-axle 7×12 shell, ~16'0" ball-to-bumper.
- **Reconfigurability** — interior breaks down and re-fits per mission; nothing welded-in.
